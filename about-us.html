about us
India is witnessing a dairy tech revolution that aims to make Indian milk amongst the best in the world in terms of quality. And Prompt, a fully indigenous brand, is leading this revolution by offering cutting-edge, conventional hardware and software solutions.
In 1995, Prompt marked the beginning of its journey in the dairy tech sector by giving India the ‘Automatic Milk Collection System’, which helped the dairies in India to digitalise and streamline their supply chain. Since then, we have come a long way. Today, from farm management and milk collection to quality analysis and preservation, Prompt offers innovative equipment and software solutions to ensure that the purity of milk is retained at every step. Most of our products and solutions are Cloud, IoT and BI-enabled which facilitates transparency as well as traceability throughout the supply chain at all levels on a real-time basis.
With our presence across 24 states, 221 districts, 55,000+ villages and a team of over 800+ employees, we have embarked on a relentless pursuit to ensure the best quality of milk reaches the people of India. And we call it, the pursuit of purity.
Vision

Transforming Indian dairy industry to substantially improve the lives of those involved in the dairy supply chain.

Mission

To provide innovative products and services that deliver, retain and enhance quality, transparency, simplicity and affordability for an effecient supply chain.

Values

With empathy as our compass, we strive to understand and address the challenges faced by individuals in the industry. We conduct business with unwavering integrity, fostering trust and transparency. Through collaboration and innovation, we aim to drive positive change, embracing sustainable practices and ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety. Our commitment extends to empowering stakeholders and making a lasting social impact, as we work towards a thriving and inclusive dairy ecosystem.
